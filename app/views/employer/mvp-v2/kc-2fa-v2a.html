To get started

Download the authenticator app

  1. Go to your mobile device's app store.
  2. Search for 'Authenticator App'
  3. Download the app (FreeOTP or Google Authenticator)

After you have downloaded the app

  1. Use the app to scan the QR code below

    2. If you can't scan the image enter the code below

    JJIU SSRZ NESH URZW M5TT QTKD NRSH ILYV

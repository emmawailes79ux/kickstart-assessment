If you're an employer looking to create jobs

Employers of all sizes can apply for funding to create jobs for 16 to 25 year olds on Universal Credit.

The funding covers:

  • 100% of the National Minimum Wage (or the National Living Wage depending on the age of the participant) for 25 hours per week for a total of 6 months
  • associated employer National Insurance contributions
  • any relevant workplace pension contributions (automatic enrolment)

Follow the step by step for employers.

If you're a Kickstart gateway looking to help employers apply for funding

A Kickstart gateway can be any type of organisation, such as a local authority, charity or trade body.

You will act as an intermediary and apply for funding on behalf of an employer.

You may also provide employability support to the young people in the job on behalf of the employer.

Follow the step by step for Kickstart gateways.

Get help with the Kickstart Scheme

Get help with your Kickstart Scheme online account and find the answers to all of your questions about the service below or by contacting us at kickstart.support@dwp.gov.uk.

To help us respond to your problems faster include:

  • Employer name registered with the Kickstart Scheme
  • Kickstart Scheme reference (KS-xxxxxxxx)
  • The name of the person you want us to contact
  • The email address the person you want us to contact
  • When the issue happened, giving us a date and time (if known)

We can help with

Getting started and signing into the service

This includes signing in, setting up your security, creating passwords, adding or amending vacancies.

If you are unsure what to do to get started, then use the “Get help with our service” link or email our support team at the address above.

Technical problems you might be having

Sometimes you may experience problems with our service. We can fix it if you report it to our team.

This might include broken links, forgotten passwords, a change of email address and problems adding or submitting vacancy details.

You can get help with the service or email our support team at kickstart.support@dwp.gov.uk


More information about the Kickstart Scheme

You can find information about the Kickstart Scheme and how to get started on GOV.UK for employers.
